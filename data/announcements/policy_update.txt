IMPORTANT: LEAVE POLICY UPDATE
Date: January 10, 2025

Attention All Employees,

Effective February 1, 2025, we are updating our leave application process:

NEW REQUIREMENTS:
1. All leave applications must be submitted at least 7 days in advance
2. Emergency leave requires manager approval within 24 hours
3. Medical certificates are now MANDATORY for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days
4. Leave balance can be checked via the HR portal

WHAT'S CHANGING:
- Email applications will no longer be accepted
- All requests must go through the HR portal
- Approval workflow now includes both manager and HR sign-off

For questions, contact HR at hrteam@company.com
